INTERNSHIP DESCRIPTION

At EY, you’ll have the chance to build a career as unique as you are, with the global scale, support, inclusive culture and technology to become the best version of you. • And we’re counting on your unique voice and perspective to help EY become even better, too. • Join us and build an exceptional experience for yourself, and a better working world for all • EY offers a variety of career paths and opportunities, and with so many options, we understand it can be hard to choose.

Your key responsibilities:
  • Building an intimate understanding of our service lines and professional services
  • Developing a foundation for the most sought-after business skills through classroom and on the job training
  • Nurturing relationships with colleagues, potentially identifying mentors who can contribute to your success
  • Discovering and adapting to our unique collaborative culture
  • Building an intimate understanding of our service lines and professional services
  • We’re looking for people who are innovative, can work in an agile way and keep pace with a rapidly changing world.
  • This is a rotational program, so you’ll learn all about a wide variety of areas and service lines specific to your market and interests such as Assurance, Consulting, and Tax. Whichever areas you visit, this is a true learning experience where you’re likely to spend time rotating around and supporting colleagues on live projects right from the start and observing conflict management and team dynamics.
